Lil' Mage

During a 2 week sprint, I created a top-down dungeon shooter with 5 levels, a Start Menu, and 2 end game windows. Drawing inspiration from Wii Play Tanks, you control a wizard through each level fighting off skeletons with bouncing fire balls, before facing the final boss.

HOC Project

During another 2 week sprint I used a stripped down Epic Learning Kit HOC base, to create a playable environment that contained Levers, Buttons, Damage, etc. Having exceeded the base requirements early on, I worked on creating more complex features that the instructors had yet to implement themselves. This included transforming between a Ball Pawn and the Player Character, creating Grappling Hooks, and introducing a Gliding mechanism.
